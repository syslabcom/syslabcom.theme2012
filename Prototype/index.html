Services

SYSLAB.COM provides services for companies, organisations and governmental institutions within the whole software lifecycle.

Assessment Services Implementation & Integration Customisation Open Source

Our assessment services are designed to provide customers with preliminary consulting services on their existing technical infrastructure with regards to their business processes and goals. The next level would be to make recommendations on integration of Open Source solutions into the existing framework. This initial assessment allows the parties involved to get a clear insight on requirements and solutions respectively, which clearly is the foundation for a successful implementation of the given solution.

Our Implementation and Integration services assist in initial deployment, with a broad range of test and quality assurance measures. One of the main service offerings within the integration phase is migration of existing contents. During implementation process, any customisation requirements that were deemed necessary during the assessment phase can be undertaken.
